History of the Project

The idea for this project was born out of the need to easily compare and analyze laptop specifications from a large dataset. Many consumers struggle to understand technical jargon or to find laptops that meet their specific needs.

This website was created as a data-driven solution, where users can:

  • Explore a wide range of laptops from different brands
  • Understand configurations through structured data
  • Make informed purchase decisions based on specs and ratings

It started as a simple CSV data exploration exercise and evolved into a full web application using Flask, Pandas, and HTML templates. The goal is to combine learning with utility—demonstrating how raw data can be transformed into an interactive, user-friendly experience.
